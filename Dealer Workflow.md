# Inventory
---
The dealer buys or exchanges the vehicle, from:
- Auto Auction
- Another Dealer
- A customer (Trade In)
- Another source

## Vehicle
---
He needs to store the information of the vehicle to be able to sale it:
- Vin
- Year
- Main Color

## Expenses
---
The **vehicle price** and other expenses need to be stored, also the **vendor** of the vehicle.

## Sale Information
---
Sale information of the vehicle should be stored to generate the forms that are needed, including:
- Buyer's Guide

# Sale
---
To enter a new sale, the inventory has to be already registered. Vehicles can be sold cash or on payments. If they are sold on payments is considered a Buy Here Pay Here sale, and the dealer has to have a license from the [[OCCC]] and follow the required registry.

## Customer
---
Customer information has to be entered, including:
- Name
- Last Name

## Co-Buyer
---
A person can buy the car and share responsibilities and rewards(?) with the customer.
