# Responsibilities
---
- Ensure sellers and holder of retail installment contracts have a motor vehicle sales finance license.
	- Dealer holding contracts (Buy Here Pay Here)
	- Dealer Assigning Contracts
	- Acceptance Companies
- A sale is considered financed when the buyer agrees to make payments over time.
# Reasons to remove license
---
- Unauthorized Documentary Fees
- Unlicensed Activity
- Excessive Itemized Charges: Texas law only allows four specific itemized charges to be included in a motor vehicle retail installment contract. (Sec. 348.005)
- Not keep adequate records of business ([Title 7, Section 84.707 of the Texas Administrative Code](https://www.law.cornell.edu/regulations/texas/7-Tex-Admin-Code-SS-84-707))
- Unlawfully repossession of collateral ([Section 348.008](https://statutes.capitol.texas.gov/Docs/FI/htm/FI.348.htm#348.008) [Sections 9.610-.622 of the Texas Business and Commerce Code](https://statutes.capitol.texas.gov/Docs/BC/htm/BC.9.htm#9.610))
- Fail to release liens on automobiles that had been paid in full. ([Sec. 348.408](https://statutes.capitol.texas.gov/Docs/FI/htm/FI.348.htm#348.408))
- Charging unauthorized inspection fees (Sec. 348.006)
- Failing to transfer title to buyer of a motor vehicle (Title 7, Section 84.707)
